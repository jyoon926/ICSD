Activities and Fundraising

We are preparing for our teams to travel to the National TSA Conference, the culmination of the year’s work. We perennially field very competitive teams, and we hope to make our school and Ithaca proud again this year. This conference, as well as other expenses such as materials and national TSA fees, require significant financial support. Team members’ families contribute by far the largest share, and we also are very grateful for support from the Ithaca City School District.

Crowdfunding

This year, IHSTSA has led various crowdfunding and fundraising programs. Our crowdfunding campaign sourced donations from a variety of generous people and small businesses around the community, and raised over $3,000 from donations from the community alone. These donors have helped our chapter raise tens of thousands of dollars over the years to help subsidize the cost for parents of paying for their children to go to the national conference.

Gertrude Hawk Chocolates

In order to extend our fundraising campaign, IHSTSA decided to pursue a new type of fundraiser, Gertrude Hawk Chocolates. Students in IHSTSA work hard to sell bars of chocolate for $1 each and earn significant profits, sometimes earning hundreds of dollars off their trip through fundraising efforts.
